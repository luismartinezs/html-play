About Doge

Doge is an Internet meme that became popular in 2013. The meme typically consists of a Shiba Inu dog accompanied by multicolored text in Comic Sans font representing a kind of internal monologue, in broken English.

Origins

The original Doge photo dates back to 2010 and features a Shiba Inu named Kabosu, adopted by Japanese kindergarten teacher Atsuko Sato. Kabosu's adorable and expressive face quickly gained popularity on the internet.

Impact on Culture

Doge has become a beloved part of internet culture, spawning countless variations and even a cryptocurrency called Dogecoin. The meme's playful and endearing nature continues to bring joy to people worldwide.
